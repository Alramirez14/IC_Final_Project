Custom Tile Downloading Tool


Below is an interactive map designed to extract tiles at a chosen zoom levels from popular web map tiles services. Available here are: ESRI Satellite Imagery, OpenTopoMap, Stadia Outdoor, and CartoDB Voyager. To begin, either select a region with the "Draw Polygon Tool" (the pentagon in the top left) or upload a geojson file.

Once you have an area selected, click the download button and choose a zoom level (within about 2 levels of the current zoom - shown in the bottom left). The image will now be downloaded. **Note: The date range feature is still in development.

Created by Alec Ramirez for GEOG 572, Oregon State University.
